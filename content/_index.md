### The Benthic Zone

**Benthic** / bĕn′thĭk / *adjective*: living in or relating to the lowest levels of the ocean or other body of water. Benthic.io is being created as a base layer in the vast ocean of information to ensure that data capable of public good remains freely available to the public, forever. The mission is to provide high-quality data that is easily accessible by modern tools and workflows and supports research, innovation, and accountability across domains that impact us all. Truth is separate from political affiliation, virtue signaling, and marketed narratives. Benthic.io will freely provide raw data to anyone who can use it, for any reason whatsoever. It is up to all of us to ensure that the truth can rise above the noise.

### Ironic Debut
In keeping with the politically agnostic nature of it's mission benthic.io enters the fray with an API collection dubbed NGOpen. The widespread abuse and fraud taking place in the current US government funding landscape has sparked some of the most politically charged debates in recent times. The aim of the NGOpen collection is to inject truth and accountability into a discourse rife with venom, hyperbole and hidden agendas. These RESTful APIs utilize [postgREST](https://docs.postgrest.org/en/v14/) (see [APIs / Docs](/apis/)) to make geospatially enhanced versions of raw goverment databases, as well as other publically available information relevant to government funding research, easily accessible to modern tools and workflows - no authentication required.

### Moving Forward
The ocean is a vast and unforgiving place rife with greed, monsters, and misinformation. This lobster is never going to quit, but I sure could use your help. Questions, comments, and suggestions aren't just welcomed they are *deperately needed*.
