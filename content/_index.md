### Welcome

Benthic.io hosts public good datasets for free. The mission is to promote the public good by enabling free access to high-quality datasets that support research, innovation, and accountability across domains that impact all of us. Truth is separate from political affiliation, virtue signaling, and marketed narratives. Benthic.io is being created to provide raw data to anyone who can use it, for any reason whatsoever. It is up to all of us to ensure that the truth can rise above the noise.

### An Ironic Debut
In keeping with the politically agnostic nature of the mission we enter the fray with a dataset dubbed NGOpen. The widespread abuse and fraud taking place in the current funding landscape has sparked some of the most politically charged debates in recent times. We aim to inject truth and accountability into a discourse rife with venom, hyperbole and hidden agendas. The APIs provide structured, queryable access to major U.S. government datasets enhanced with geocoding, census linkages, reference tables, and more. Whether you are mapping, doing entity linking, trend analysis, or transparency work, our PostgREST APIs provide clean JSON endpoints with full spatial filtering capabilities - no authentication required. Check out the [documentation](/apis/).
